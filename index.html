Project Details

The LaserCube

A powerful and compact laser projector originally built for DJs and light shows that operates with its own unique software, LaserOS. This innovative technology was designed by Wicked Lasers, a global leader in laser technology.

(Visit their website to learn more!)

Our Client

Chris Gerlach, a faculty member of the New Media Institute (NMI) who seeks innovative, visually engaging solutions to enhance live presentations and experiences for NMI-related events.

Our Solution

  • Development of custom graphics and animations that can be projected with the LaserCube
  • Experimental media to be paired with laser projection
  • A streamlined system for our client to use the LaserCube on stage

Asset Library

Our comprehensive library of stage-ready assets, compatible with LaserOS, features 2D and 3D graphics that are tailored for the NMI and creation of immersive, engaging experiences.
